About Me

I am Josh Williams, and I am a software developer from Kent, Washington. Prior to deciding to become a programmer, I worked as a musician. I spent 6 years working in cruise ship bands, as a guitarist and later on as a music manager. For the past couple of years I have taught private music lessons and done freelance work as a guitarist. These experiences have given me strong organizational skills, a high level of attention to detail, and made me adept at working with teams. I decided to learn Software Development because I wanted to do work that allowed me to focus on one bigger picture rather than focus on taking as many small gigs and jobs as possible. I chose this field, because I like the intersection of creativity and logic it offers, and I enjoy challenges. I look forward to finding a role where I can continue to grow as a developer and contribute meaningfully to a great team.
